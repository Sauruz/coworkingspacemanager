A) What’s in here and what’s not? - top

Coworking Space Manager (CSM) offers a tool to manage you coworking space. You can keep track of members, workplaces and plans. All the functionalities are listed here below.

Currenly there is no integration with a payment provider. The plan is to include this in further releases, depending on the wishes of the users of this plugin. Also, new members can’t sign up, an admin have to add them in the plugin. This is done for security reasons and to keep the admin in control. Again, this is open for discussion. If you really need users to sign up without admin interference, send me a message. Multilanguage is not supported yet, but might be in the next release.

B) Installation - top

Log in to your WP admin area and go to Plugins > Add New. Browse to the plugin archive. Now select the coworkingspacemanager.zip you can find in your package. Then click `Install Now` and the plugin will be installed shortly.

C) Dashboard - top

After installation you’ll see Coworking Space in the left menu in the WP admin area. If you click on the button you’ll go to the admin area. This should look similar like this:

Here you can find all statistics of your coworking space. Also, you can find the url to the members area in the blue bar. This is the area where members can login and will be explained here.

D) All members - top

If you click on `All members` in the admin menu, you’ll find a overview with all the registered members. CSM members are basically WP members of the type csm-members. Admins that can login to your WP member area, can make a reservation for a workplace as well. You’ll notice that admins will have a green (admin) behind their names. CSM members can be deleted, admins can not. If you would like to delete an admin you can do it the same way as usual under `Users` in the admin menu.

`Active` members are the members that are currently in the coworking space. `Upcoming` members are members that have made a reservervation for the future and `Inactive` members have no upcoming or active memberships.

E) Members details - top

If you click in `All members` on a name, you’ll go to the details of a member. Here you can see the membership history, register as payment, add a new membership for the user of edit his/her credentials.

F) All memberships - top

Here you can see al the memberships of all members. You can add a membership for a member on this page as well. This is also possible if you go to a member detail page.

G) Calendar - top

In the calendar you can see in a, well, calendar who is in the coworking space and when.

H) Settings - top

Edit your settings for your coworking space. If you upload a logo, this will the logo members will see if they log in to the coworking space backend.

I) Plans - top

Plans are the prices and timespan for a `workplace`. You always need at least 1 workplace to add a plan. So let’s assume you’ve got a couple of hot desks, you can rent them out for a day, a week or whatever you’d like.

J) Workplaces - top

At workplaces you can add your desk types. You can add for example hot desks, private offices or private desks. You can also specify the capacity.

K) Members Area - top

If you look at your CSM Dashboard, you can see a blue box that contains an url. This is the link where members of your coworking space can log in. This url depends on your permalink WP settings. Here a member can login and edit his/her settings. If you have enabled ‘Users can add their own membership in the members area` in `Settings`, users can add their own membership in the members area.
